**Tax & VAT Advisory**

The Service Provider shall provide tax advisory services, including:
- Tax compliance advisory and VAT/GST-related support (advisory only, not tax filing)
- Tax planning and optimization strategies
- VAT/GST registration and compliance guidance
- Transfer pricing advisory
- Tax audit support and documentation

**IMPORTANT:** The Service Provider does not provide tax filing services unless explicitly stated in a separate engagement. The Client remains solely responsible for compliance with all applicable tax laws and regulations in their jurisdiction.
